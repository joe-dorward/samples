Class Attendance Rules

You are all adults, therefore:

1. You may come to class at anytime -- if you arrive more than 20 minutes after the start of class, you will will be marked absent for the first hour.

2. You may leave class at anytime -- if you leave class more than 20 minutes before the end of class, you will be marked absent for the last hour.

The only exception -- if I dismiss you, or the class.

You will loose ¼% from your final grade for each hour that you miss from class.
