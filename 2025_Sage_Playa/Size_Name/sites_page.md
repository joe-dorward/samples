The **Sites** page is the 'parent' page for **Site** management, and it contains:
- A list of **Sites** in a columned widget
- The **Export** button - organisational users will click this button to export the list of **Sites** as an **Excel** spreadsheet
- The **Filter** button - organisational users will click this button to filter the list of users in the columned widget

Organisational users will filter the list of **Sites** by **Name** to make it easier for them to find a specific **Site**.

The **Sites** page is dynamic. Depending on organisational type, specific organisation, and role, a user will see different **Sites** pages. For example:

- **Sage Operating Company** users (with the appropriate role) will see the list of all **Sites** added to their **Sage Operating Company**
- **Partner** organisation users (with the appropriate role) will see the list of all **Sites** added by other users of their **Partner** organisation
- **Customer** organisation users (with the appropriate role) will see the list of all **Sites** added to their **Customer** organisation

The list of **Sites** shows:
- The **Name** of the **Site**
- The name of the **Customer** organisation for which the **Site** was added
- The name of the **Product** hosted by the **Site**
- The **Version** of the **Product** hosted by the **Site**
- The **Status** of the **Site**
- A **Tag** associated with the **Site**

Organisational users will click the **Name** of a **Site** to open the **SiteDetails** page of that **Site**.
